<p><ul><li>DLI-CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition)</li><li>DLI-ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DLI vaporizers are essential in the semiconductor and materials industries, serving two primary market types: DLI-CVD and DLI-ALD. DLI-CVD (Direct Liquid Injection Chemical Vapor Deposition) allows for precise deposition of thin films by introducing liquid precursors into a gaseous state, facilitating uniform coatings. Conversely, DLI-ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition) enables the formation of atomic-scale layers through sequential self-limiting chemical reactions, providing exceptional control over thickness and composition. Both techniques are crucial for advancing nanotechnology and electronic applications.</p></p>
