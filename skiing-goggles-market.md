<p><p>The skiing goggles market features three main lens types: cylindrical, spherical, and toric. Cylindrical lenses are flat and offer a wide field of vision but may distort peripheral views. Spherical lenses, curved in both directions, enhance visibility and reduce glare, providing a more natural view. Toric lenses combine both shapes, catering to those needing prescription eyewear while optimizing clarity and comfort. Each type caters to different skiing preferences, ensuring skiers can choose based on their visibility requirements and style.</p></p>
