<p><p>The biosensor system market can be categorized into three main types: thermal, electrochemical, and optical. Thermal biosensors measure changes in temperature related to biochemical reactions, making them useful for detecting microbial activity. Electrochemical biosensors utilize electrical signals to monitor chemical interactions, often employed in glucose monitoring and environmental testing. Optical biosensors rely on light-based detection methods, such as fluorescence or absorbance, to analyze biological samples. Each type offers unique advantages suited to specific applications in healthcare, food safety, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
