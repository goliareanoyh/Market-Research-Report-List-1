<p><p>A Nasal Pillow Mask is a type of CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) device used primarily for the treatment of obstructive sleep apnea. This mask design is favored for its lightweight and minimally invasive structure, making it a popular choice among users who may find full face masks cumbersome.</p><p>The Nasal Pillow Mask Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period.
