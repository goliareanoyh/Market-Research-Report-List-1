Monomers serve as building blocks for polymer chains, enabling customization of properties for various applications. The 'others' category includes additives and formulations that improve performance characteristics or application methods. Together, these types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, enhancing product features like UV resistance, adhesion, and elasticity.</p></p>
