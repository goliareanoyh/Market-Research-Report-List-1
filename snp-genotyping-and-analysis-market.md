<p><p>SNP genotyping and analysis encompass various methodologies for detecting single nucleotide polymorphisms (SNPs), critical for genetic research and personalized medicine. Dynamic Allele-Specific Hybridization utilizes specific probes to assess SNP variations by measuring hybridization kinetics. Molecular Beacons are fluorescent probes that detect SNPs based on changes in fluorescence upon hybridization with target sequences. SNP Microarrays enable the simultaneous analysis of thousands of SNPs on a single chip, offering high-throughput genotyping for large-scale studies. Together, they enhance genetic insight and application.</p></p>
