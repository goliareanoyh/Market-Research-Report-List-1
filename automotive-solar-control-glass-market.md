<p><ul><li>Absorbing Solar Control Glass</li><li>Reflective Solar Control Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive solar control glass market comprises two main types: absorbing and reflective solar control glass. Absorbing solar control glass utilizes specialized materials to absorb sunlight and reduce interior heat, enhancing passenger comfort. In contrast, reflective solar control glass features a coating that reflects solar radiation, minimizing heat buildup while maintaining visibility. Both types aim to improve energy efficiency in vehicles, reduce reliance on air conditioning, and enhance overall driving experience by managing solar heat gain effectively.</p></p>
