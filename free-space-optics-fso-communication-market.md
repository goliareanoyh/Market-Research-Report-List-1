<p><p>Free Space Optics (FSO) communication encompasses various market segments critical for optical transmission systems. Transmitters convert electrical signals into optical signals, while receivers detect and transform optical signals back into electrical formats. Modulators manipulate the optical signals for efficient data transmission, and demodulators decode the received signals. Encoders prepare the data for transmission by formatting it appropriately, whereas decoders retrieve the original data from the received signals, ensuring seamless communication. Together, these components enable effective FSO communication solutions.</p></p>
