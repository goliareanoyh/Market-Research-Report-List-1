Flow Type quantifies the airflow necessary to maintain a specific pressure difference, providing insight into leakage rates.</p><p>3. Gauge Pressure Type measures static air pressure, detecting variations that indicate potential leaks.</p><p>4. Other types include hybrid systems or advanced technology testers that combine multiple measurement principles for enhanced accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
