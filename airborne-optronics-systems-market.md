<p><p>Airborne Optronics Systems encompass multispectral and hyperspectral markets, each serving distinct applications. Multispectral systems capture data across a limited range of wavelengths, typically focusing on visible and near-infrared light, making them suitable for reconnaissance and environmental monitoring. In contrast, hyperspectral systems analyze a broader spectrum, detecting hundreds of wavelengths, which enhances material identification and detailed analysis in fields like agriculture and mineralogy. Both systems play crucial roles in intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) operations, enhancing situational awareness.</p></p>
