<p><p>The milking machine market is categorized into automatic and semi-automatic systems. Automatic milking machines fully mechanize the milking process, allowing cows to self-attach machines, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs. These systems often include advanced monitoring technology for milk quality and herd management. Semi-automatic milking machines require some manual intervention, combining efficiency with operator control. They are often more cost-effective for smaller dairies while still improving milking speed and hygiene compared to traditional methods.</p></p>
