<p><p>The osteoarthritis drugs market can be categorized into three main types: oral, injection, and external formulations. Oral drugs, such as analgesics and nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), provide systemic relief from pain and inflammation. Injection treatments, including corticosteroids and hyaluronic acid, deliver targeted relief directly into the affected joints, offering more immediate effects. External applications, like topical creams and patches, focus on localized pain management, providing convenience and targeted action without systemic exposure. Each type caters to varying patient preferences and therapeutic needs.</p></p>
