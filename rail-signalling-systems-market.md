<p><strong>The Rail Signalling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Train Control</li><li>Communication Based Train Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail signalling systems can be categorized into two main types: Traditional Train Control and Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC). Traditional Train Control relies on fixed signals and trackside equipment to manage train movements, ensuring safety and efficiency through established protocols. In contrast, CBTC utilizes modern communication technologies to enable real-time data exchange between trains and control centers, allowing for more flexible and responsive train operations. This approach enhances capacity, reduces headways, and improves overall safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
