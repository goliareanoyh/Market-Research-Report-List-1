<p><strong>The UV-LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-A LED</li><li>UV-B LED</li><li>UV-C LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-LED market is categorized into three main types: UV-A, UV-B, and UV-C. UV-A LEDs, typically used in curing processes and medical applications, emit light in the 320-400 nm range. UV-B LEDs, emitting 280-320 nm, are primarily utilized in therapeutic and horticultural applications, promoting plant growth and skin treatments. UV-C LEDs, with wavelengths between 200-280 nm, are essential for disinfection and sterilization, effectively eliminating pathogens and viruses, thereby gaining significant traction in healthcare and public safety sectors.</p></p>
