<p><p>The snow sweeper market consists of two primary types: rotary snow sweepers and wedge snow sweepers. Rotary snow sweepers utilize rotating brushes to efficiently clear snow from surfaces, making them ideal for urban areas and large parking lots. In contrast, wedge snow sweepers feature a angled design that compacts and pushes snow aside, making them suitable for narrow pathways and sidewalks. Both types serve essential roles in snow removal, enhancing safety and accessibility during winter months.</p></p>
