<p><ul><li>Compressor Type Dehumidifiers</li><li>Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential portable dehumidifier market consists of two main types: compressor type and desiccant type dehumidifiers. Compressor type dehumidifiers work by drawing in humid air, cooling it to condense moisture, and then reheating it before releasing it back into the room, making them efficient for warmer climates. In contrast, desiccant type dehumidifiers utilize moisture-absorbing materials to capture humidity, performing well in lower temperatures. Both types cater to different environmental needs, offering homeowners options for managing indoor humidity effectively.</p></p>
