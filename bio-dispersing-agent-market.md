<p><ul><li>Anionic Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bio-dispersing agent market is categorized into three primary types: anionic, cationic, and others. Anionic bio-dispersing agents carry a negative charge, facilitating the dispersion of particles in water-based systems, often used in paints and coatings. Cationic agents, with a positive charge, are effective in enhancing stability and adhesion in various applications, including textiles and personal care products. "Others" encompasses non-ionic and amphoteric types, which offer versatile dispersion properties across different formulations and industries.</p></p>
