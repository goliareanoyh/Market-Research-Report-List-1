<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Color Sorter Machine</li><li>Belt-Type Color Sorter Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical sorters market comprises two primary types: chute-type color sorters and belt-type color sorters. Chute-type color sorters utilize a vertical drop mechanism, efficiently sorting materials as they fall through a chute, making them suitable for bulk products like grains and nuts. In contrast, belt-type color sorters move products along a conveyor belt, enabling thorough inspection and sorting for a broader range of items, including smaller or fragile products, offering flexibility and precision in processing.</p></p>
