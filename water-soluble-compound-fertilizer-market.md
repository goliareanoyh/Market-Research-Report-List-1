<p><p>The water-soluble compound fertilizer market is divided into two main types: liquid fertilizers and solid fertilizers. Liquid fertilizers are concentrated solutions that dissolve quickly in water, making them easy to apply through irrigation systems or foliar sprays, promoting rapid nutrient absorption. Solid fertilizers, on the other hand, are granulated or powdered forms that dissolve in water before application. Both types provide essential nutrients for plants, enhancing growth and yield, and are suitable for a variety of agricultural practices.</p></p>
