<p><p>The construction helmet market comprises various types designed to ensure worker safety in hazardous environments. ABS protective helmets offer high impact resistance and durability, making them suitable for general construction work. HDPE protective helmets are lightweight and provide excellent protection against impacts while being cost-effective. FRP protective helmets are made from fiberglass-reinforced plastic, offering superior strength and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for environments with electrical hazards or corrosive substances. Each type serves specific industry needs and safety standards.</p></p>
