<p><p>The photovoltaic solar panel market is primarily categorized into three types: monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film panels. Monocrystalline panels are made from single crystal structures, offering high efficiency and space-saving designs but at a higher cost. Polycrystalline panels consist of multiple crystal structures, providing a cost-effective solution with slightly lower efficiency. Thin-film panels, made by layering photovoltaic materials, are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for diverse applications, though they typically have lower efficiency and require more space.</p></p>
