<p><p>The lymphedema diagnostics market includes various imaging and analysis techniques. Lymphoscintigraphy uses radioactive isotopes to visualize lymphatic function. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides detailed soft tissue images, while Ultrasound Imaging assesses lymphatic swelling through sound waves. Computed Tomography (CT) offers cross-sectional images for structural evaluation, and X-Ray Lymphography visualizes lymphatic vessels using contrast agents. Bioimpedance Analysis measures tissue fluid levels to assess lymphedema, and Near-Infrared Fluorescence Imaging utilizes fluorescent dyes for real-time lymphatic visualization, enhancing diagnosis accuracy.</p></p>
