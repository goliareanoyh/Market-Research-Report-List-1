<p><strong>The RO Pure Water Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU RO Pure Water Machine</li><li>POE RO Pure Water Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO Pure Water Machine market is primarily segmented into Point of Use (POU) and Point of Entry (POE) systems. POU RO machines are designed for installation at specific water consumption points, providing purified water directly to faucets or dispensers. They are ideal for residential and small-scale applications. Conversely, POE RO machines are installed at the main water supply entry, treating all incoming water for an entire household or facility, making them suitable for larger scale needs and ensuring comprehensive filtration.</p></p>
