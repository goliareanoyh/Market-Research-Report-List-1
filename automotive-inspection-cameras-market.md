<p><p>The automotive inspection cameras market comprises three main types: area scan cameras, line scan cameras, and others. Area scan cameras capture images of a complete area simultaneously, making them ideal for inspecting large components quickly. Line scan cameras, on the other hand, capture images one line at a time, offering high-resolution details, perfect for detailed inspections of long parts or surfaces. "Others" may include specialized cameras, such as 3D or thermal imaging systems, catering to unique inspection requirements within the automotive industry.</p></p>
