<p><p>The Bilgewater Separator market comprises several types designed to treat oily water from ships. Centrifugal Bilgewater Separators utilize centrifugal force to separate oil from water. Electrochemical separators employ electrical methods to break down contaminants. Gravity Bilgewater Separators rely on density differences for separation, allowing oil to rise and be removed. Bioremediation Bilgewater Separators use biological processes to degrade oil and other pollutants, effectively cleaning the bilgewater and reducing environmental impact. Each type offers distinct advantages based on operational needs.</p></p>
