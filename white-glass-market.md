<p><p>The White Glass market is categorized into high porosity and low porosity segments based on their structural characteristics. High porosity white glass features increased void spaces, allowing for enhanced light diffusion and thermal insulation, making it suitable for applications like art, architectural elements, and lighting fixtures. Conversely, low porosity white glass has fewer voids, resulting in greater density and strength, making it ideal for high-impact uses such as packaging, laboratory glassware, and durable consumer products. These distinctions cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
