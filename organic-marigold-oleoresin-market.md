<p><p>The Organic Marigold Oleoresin market comprises two primary extraction methods: Solvent Extraction and Supercritical Fluid Extraction (SFE). Solvent Extraction utilizes organic solvents to dissolve and extract oleoresins from marigold flowers, yielding concentrated flavors and colors. In contrast, SFE employs supercritical gases, typically carbon dioxide, to extract compounds without residual solvents, resulting in a purer product. Both methods cater to different consumer needs, with SFE often preferred for its environmental benefits and higher purity, appealing to health-conscious markets.</p></p>
