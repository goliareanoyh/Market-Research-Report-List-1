<p><p>The lactase market can be categorized into two main types: neutral lactase and acid lactase. Neutral lactase functions optimally at a higher pH, making it suitable for lactose-free dairy products where a mild flavor is desired, such as in milk and yogurts. In contrast, acid lactase is designed to operate in more acidic environments, ideal for products like cheese and fermented foods. Both types cater to the growing demand for lactose intolerance solutions, enhancing product diversity and consumer accessibility.</p></p>
