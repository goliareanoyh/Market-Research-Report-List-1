<p><ul><li>Electric Lighter</li><li>Fuel Lighter</li><li>Plasma Lighter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The survival lighter market encompasses three main types: electric lighters, fuel lighters, and plasma lighters. Electric lighters use a rechargeable battery to generate heat for igniting materials, offering convenience and environmentally friendly use. Fuel lighters, typically using butane or lighter fluid, are more traditional and widely accessible, favored for their reliability in various weather conditions. Plasma lighters utilize electric arcs for ignition, providing windproof, flameless operation, ideal for outdoor enthusiasts seeking durability and efficiency in challenging environments.</p></p>
