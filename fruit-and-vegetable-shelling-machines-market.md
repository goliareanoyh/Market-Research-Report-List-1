<p><p>The fruit and vegetable shelling machines market is primarily divided into two types: vertical and horizontal shelling machines. Vertical type shelling machines utilize gravity to facilitate the shelling process, making them suitable for smaller operations or specific fruits and vegetables. In contrast, horizontal type shelling machines operate using a more versatile design that allows for higher capacity and efficiency, accommodating larger volumes and different product sizes. Both types aim to enhance productivity, reduce labor costs, and improve shelling quality.</p></p>
