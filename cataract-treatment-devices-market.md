<p><p>The cataract treatment devices market includes three primary types: Intraocular Lenses (IOLs), Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices (OVDs), and Phacoemulsification Equipment. IOLs are artificial lenses implanted during cataract surgery to restore clarity of vision. OVDs are gel-like substances utilized during surgery to maintain eye shape and protect tissues. Phacoemulsification Equipment employs ultrasound technology to break down cataracts, facilitating their removal. Together, these devices enhance surgical outcomes and improve patient experiences in cataract treatment.</p></p>
