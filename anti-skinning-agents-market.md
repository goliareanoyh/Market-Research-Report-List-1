<p><p>Anti-skinning agents are additives employed in coatings and paints to prevent the formation of a skin or film on the surface during drying. Key market types include oximes and phenols. Oximes, such as butanone oxime, effectively inhibit skin formation by reacting with free radicals, enhancing film formation and durability. Phenols, like phenol and its derivatives, provide similar functions, offering stability and improved appearance. Both types contribute to the overall quality and longevity of coatings, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>
