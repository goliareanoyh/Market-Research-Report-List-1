<p><p>Ceramic infrared heaters are categorized into three market types based on their wavelength emissions: short wave, medium wave, and long wave. Short wave heaters emit high-intensity heat, providing immediate warmth and are ideal for industrial applications. Medium wave heaters offer a balanced heat output, suitable for comfort heating in commercial spaces. Long wave heaters emit lower intensity heat, ideal for providing consistent warmth in residential settings. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to various heating needs in different environments.</p></p>
